Don't Stress. We'll find your pet.

Losing a pet can be one of the most devastating feelings. To a pet owner, a pet is just another member of the family. Why waste hours and hours posting flyers all around town with little to no results when technology is on your side.


Make finding your pet as easy as it can be.

Here at Off The Chain, we've compiled databases from all local animal intake shelters, individuals who have found pets, pounds, etc. into an easy to search app that lets pet owners search for found pets online or post a lost pet to the database.
The database is updated daily with new pet intakes and owners will be notified of any potential matches. Individuals who have found pets can also post the pet to the database.
